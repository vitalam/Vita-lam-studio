## 0B. v10 — dual positioning: premium AND economical (no compromise)

Resolution of the apparent conflict: **premium describes the output; economical describes the structure** — different axes, so both can be maximised. On-page devices: (1) section heading 「頂級規格。經濟結構。」 with the fused line 「慳嘅係浪費，唔係質素。」; (2) twin manifesto cards — 經濟，因為 (skip what you don't need, no agency overhead/headcount/rent, every quote line is real work) vs 頂級，因為 (SFC-grade rigour, patent-pending Firewall, painter's eye, warranty, 100% ownership — agency-grade blocks, not budget substitutes); (3) a **live agency-equivalent meter** in the shopping list and on the quotation: 同等範圍・代理行情 ≈ 2.5–5× the one-off total (struck through) shown above 你嘅價 — the customer's number reads economical against a premium anchor in the same glance. Factual basis: the researched 2.5–5× agency/freelancer gap (§2.1). Language rule: never "cheap/平", never "discount/折" — only waste-removal and 禮遇.

## 1. Pricing strategy in one paragraph (historical tier framing)

List prices sit at roughly the **75th percentile of the Hong Kong freelance market** — above the freelance average, below every agency — justified by a bundle most quotes quietly exclude (deployment, domain, security, SSL, backups, 30-day warranty, full-code handover, AI-ready design playbook). A gated **founding-client rate** drops 30–45% below list, landing **at or below the market median**, so budget clients can enter without repositioning the brand. The list price is always displayed as the real price; the discount is always shown struck-through against it (anchoring), gated (2 slots/month, 50% upfront, portfolio + testimonial rights), and framed as mutual skin-in-the-game.

## 2. Market data (researched July 2026)

### 2.1 Hong Kong project pricing

| Scope | HK freelance range | HK agency range |
|---|---|---|
| Brochure site (1–5p) | HK$3,000–20,000 (SME average ≈ HK$8,000) | HK$20,000–65,000+ |
| Custom site + CMS (5–20p) | HK$20,000–100,000 | HK$65,000–235,000+ (custom up to HK$588,500) |
| E-commerce | HK$40,000–120,000 | HK$150,000–300,000+ |
| Custom web app with auth | HK$60,000–200,000 | HK$150,000–780,000+ |

Agencies charge roughly **2.5–5×** freelancers for equivalent scope. Senior fullstack hourly in HK: HK$330–510; lead HK$510–720+. Singapore (closest peer) tracks HK closely.

### 2.2 Add-on markets
Domain .com ≈ HK$235/yr (.hk HK$1,000–2,190); quality managed hosting HK$190–550/mo; maintenance retainers HK$300–3,000+/mo locally, US$35–500/mo globally for SMB sites.

### 2.3 AI integration market
- SMB chatbot SaaS band: **US$30–150/mo** (Chatbase $40, CustomGPT $89, Tidio+Lyro ~$70–120 all-in; Intercom Fin $0.99/resolution).
- Custom freelance builds: FAQ/RAG bot **US$1.5k–3k**; with integrations **US$3.5k–7k**; agencies US$5k–50k (SG agencies SGD 15k–35k ≈ HK$88k–205k).
- Token COGS via OpenRouter (no markup, 5.5% credit fee): a typical SMB bot (300–500 chats/mo) costs **HK$30–200/mo** in tokens. White-label resellers charge end clients US$99–499/mo — 30–70% gross margins are the market norm; the fee buys management, not tokens.

**API cost guardrail:** token costs are contractually excluded from all fixed fees — model prices move; the margin shouldn't.

### 3.3 Care plan — one price, all-inclusive

**HK$1,880/mo**: hosting, SSL, backups, monitoring, **security patching**, updates, 2 hrs changes, monthly report, human reply ≤1 working day. Extra hours HK$650/hr. Cancel anytime.

Rationale: (a) sits mid-market (HK retainers HK$300–3,000+) yet reads generous because everything is included; (b) **security is never a paid add-on** — selling protection separately creates a "pay me or stay vulnerable" liability that contradicts the ownership/honesty brand (site line: 保安唔係加購項目。係標準配備。); (c) tiered response times were dropped for the same reason — "pay more or I reply slower" is the same perverse incentive; (d) simplicity itself converts: one number, no comparison anxiety. Trade-off accepted: the old HK$3,800 Priority tier's revenue is foregone publicly; heavy clients get custom retainers quoted privately.

## 4. The founding-client mechanism

- **Why it exists:** captures budget demand (competing with the HK$3k–20k offshore/budget floor) without lowering the brand's real prices; converts early clients into the missing asset — testimonials and case studies.
- **Gates that protect the anchor:** 2 slots/month · 50% upfront · portfolio + testimonial rights · always displayed against the struck-through list price · framed as a trade, never a sale ("原價是真實價格 — 折扣是雙方先投入的誠意").
- **Exit plan:** as testimonials accumulate, tighten slots or retire the tier; never let the promo become the perceived base price.

## 5. Anchoring & presentation (research-backed)

- The agency range (HK$65,000–588,000) is stated **before** any of Vita's prices — first number seen frames everything after (Simon-Kucher; Emerald 2024 decoy study).
- Three tiers with the middle highlighted; premium visible to anchor high.
- Publishing prices at all is itself positioning: TrustRadius (Apr 2025) found pricing is the #1 thing B2B buyers want from vendors; Gartner (Jun 2025): 61% don't want to talk to a salesperson first. Hidden pricing loses shortlists — hence the site's line: "the numbers are right here. Radical, apparently."
- USD equivalents shown small for international visitors; HKD is primary.

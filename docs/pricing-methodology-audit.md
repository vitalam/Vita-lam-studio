### §1 目的 Purpose

1.1 本文件說明本公司公開價目表上所有價格嘅計算方法、數據來源及查核程序。
*This document sets out how every price on our published rate card is derived, sourced, and verifiable.*

1.2 本文件旨在為本公司之比較性價格聲稱提供實證基礎（《商品說明條例》第 362 章之精神）。
*It substantiates our comparative pricing claims in the spirit of the Trade Descriptions Ordinance (Cap. 362).*

### §2 定價公式 Pricing Formula

2.1 所有服務（一次性收費）All fees, one-off：

**P = max（ 0.50 × 市場公允價值下限,　HK$1,280,　法定最低工資 × 預估工時 ）**
*P = max( 0.50 × fair-value floor, HK$1,280, statutory minimum wage × estimated hours )*

2.2 **五折目標 The 50% target**：所有模組以公允價值下限之 50% 為定價目標，並藉 §8 之公開協作程序持續校準。
*Every module targets 50% of the fair-value floor, continuously calibrated through the open collaboration procedure in §8.*

2.3 **最低收費 Minimum charge**：每模組最低 HK$1,280。*Each module carries a HK$1,280 minimum.*

2.4 **工資下限 Wage floor**：價格在任何情況下不低於法定最低工資乘以該模組之公開預估工時。現行法定最低工資為每小時 HK$43.10（2026 年 5 月 1 日起，勞工處）。各模組預估工時載於附錄 A，並隨每週基準一併公開。
*Prices never fall below the statutory minimum wage (HK$43.10/hour from 1 May 2026, Labour Department) multiplied by the module's disclosed estimated hours (Appendix A), published with each weekly benchmark.*

2.5 品牌尾數調整（x,800／x,880／x80）只可向下或持平，且不得跌穿 §2.3 及 §2.4 之下限。
*Brand price-endings round at or below the formula output, never below the §2.3–2.4 floors.*

2.6 **範圍與最低收費之一致性 Minimum-charge consistency**：模組範圍須訂定至其同等範圍公允下限不低於 §2.3 之最低收費（例：修改請求定義為至 3 小時開發），以維持 §2.2 定價目標之一致性。
*Module scopes are defined so their equivalent-scope floor is never below the §2.3 minimum (e.g., a change request = up to 3 dev-hours), keeping the §2.2 pricing target consistent.*

2.7 **不設月費 No recurring monthly fees**：持續服務以定期預繳加購計劃（SiteCare+，24 個月）發售，按 §2.1 公式以多年期維護行情下限計價；只限項目上線時或其後 60 日內加購；期滿按當時基準價續期；可隨網站轉讓。
*Ongoing service is sold as a fixed-term prepaid add-on (SiteCare+, 24 months) priced by §2.1 against multi-year maintenance floors; attachable only at launch or within 60 days; renewable at the then-current benchmark price; transferable with the site.*

2.8 **計劃內承保服務費率 Covered-service rates under a plan**：預繳計劃內之服務（如 SiteCare+ 會員修改請求，每小時 HK$140，不限次數，優先隊列）屬計劃承保範圍之服務費，並非獨立模組，故不適用 §2.3 每模組最低收費；惟仍須符合 §2.4 工資下限（$140/h ＞ $43.10/h ✓）。非計劃客戶之修改請求則為獨立模組，適用全部公式。
*Services rendered under a prepaid plan (e.g., SiteCare+ member revisions at HK$140/hour, unlimited, priority queue) are covered-service fees, not standalone modules — the §2.3 module minimum does not apply, but the §2.4 wage floor still does ($140/h > $43.10/h ✓). Non-plan change requests are standalone modules subject to the full formula.*

### §3 定義 Definitions

3.1 **公允價值下限 Fair-value floor**：就同等範圍服務，獨立公開香港報價區間之最低位。
*The lowest end of the independently published Hong Kong price range for equivalent scope.*

3.2 **同等範圍 Equivalent scope（Q=1 訂製級）**：同時符合以下全部條件之服務——(a) 全訂製程式碼，非模板或頁面產生器；(b) 原始碼完整交付；(c) AI-ready 技術文件；(d) 包含 CMS、基礎 SEO、SSL 及部署。
*(a) fully custom code, no templates/builders; (b) full source-code handover; (c) AI-ready documentation; (d) CMS, baseline SEO, SSL and deployment included.*

3.3 不符合 §3.2 之報價（模板方案、按用戶收費之 SaaS 授權）一律剔除，不作基準。
*Quotes failing §3.2 (template offers, per-user SaaS licences) are excluded from benchmarking.*

### §4 基準程序 Benchmark Procedure

4.1 **頻率 Cadence**：逢星期一重新檢索及重算。價目表註明「基準更新」日期。
*Re-collected and recomputed every Monday; the rate card displays its refresh date.*

4.2 **樣本 Sample**：每一服務類別以不少於三個可信公開價格點建立區間。
*Each service category requires ≥3 credible published price points.*

4.3 **下限推導 Floor derivation**：剔除不符 §3.2 之報價後，取符合同等範圍之最低公開報價為下限。
*After §3.2 exclusions, the lowest remaining published quote becomes the floor.*

4.4 **穩定帶 Stability band**：下限變動少於 5% 不觸發調價，變動照錄於週報。
*Floor moves under 5% do not trigger repricing but are logged.*

4.5 **報價鎖定 Quote lock**：所有報價鎖定 14 日，以報價日與簽約日兩者中對客戶較優者為準。
*All quotes lock for 14 days at the client-favourable price.*

### §6 協作範圍及除外 Collaboration Scope & Exclusions

6.1 §2 定價目標及 §8 定價協作邀請適用於**全部**服務模組，包括訂閱及專業審視服務；唯一除外見 §6.2。
*The §2 pricing target and the §8 collaboration invitation apply to **all** service modules, including subscriptions and professional-review services; the sole exclusion is §6.2.*

6.2 **除外：AI 整合**：採用專利申請中之專有技術（AI Logic Firewall），市場無同等服務可作比較，故不納入 §8 定價協作覆核；其價格仍按 §2 公式以參考基準計算並公開。
*Exclusion — AI integration: proprietary patent-pending IP with no market equivalent, hence outside the §8 collaborative review; its price still follows the §2 formula against a reference benchmark and is published.*

6.3 **免費同行審視 Free peer review**：本公司不提供收費合規服務。每位客戶獲一次免費商業政策同行審視，由 HKCGI 認證人員以同行經驗交流形式進行——**並非顧問或法律意見**；此項不收費，故不屬 §2 公式及本程序之範圍。
*We sell no paid compliance services. Every client receives one complimentary business-policy peer review by an HKCGI-credentialed professional, shared as peer experience — **not consultancy or legal advice**; being free, it sits outside the §2 formula and this procedure.*

### §8 定價協作邀請 Pricing Collaboration Invitation

8.1 定價唔係單方面聲明，而係一個開放程序：誠邀任何人（客戶、同行、公眾）提交同等範圍（§3.2）之香港市場價格資訊——不論高於或低於現行基準——連同來源出處。
*Pricing here is an open process, not a unilateral claim: anyone — clients, peers, the public — is invited to submit Hong Kong market pricing information for equivalent scope (§3.2), above or below the current benchmark, with its source.*

8.2 所有提交均於下一個每週更新內覆核；如成立，相應下限隨即調整，並記入變更紀錄及週報。
*Every submission is reviewed at the next weekly refresh; if valid, the floor adjusts and the change is logged in the change-log and weekly report.*

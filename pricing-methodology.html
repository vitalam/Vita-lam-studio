定價方法及實證聲明Pricing Methodology & Substantiation Statement

版本：1.4 ・ 生效日：2026-07-13 ・ 發布：VITA LAM (G Entertainment Ltd)
本文件為公開文件，供客戶及任何第三方查核；並誠邀任何人提交市場價格資訊，參與校準（§8）。 Version: 1.4 · Effective: 2026-07-13 · Published by: VITA LAM (G Entertainment Ltd)
This is a public document, maintained for client and third-party audit — and an open invitation to submit market pricing information (§8).

§1 目的§1 Purpose

1.1本文件說明本公司公開價目表上所有價格嘅計算方法、數據來源及查核程序。This document sets out how every price on our published rate card is derived, sourced, and verifiable.

1.2本文件旨在為本公司之比較性價格聲稱提供實證基礎（《商品說明條例》第 362 章之精神）。It substantiates our comparative pricing claims in the spirit of the Trade Descriptions Ordinance (Cap. 362).

§2 定價公式§2 Pricing Formula

2.1所有服務（一次性收費）：All fees, one-off:

P = max（ 0.50 × 市場公允價值下限,　HK$1,280,　法定最低工資 × 預估工時 ） P = max( 0.50 × fair-value floor, HK$1,280, statutory minimum wage × estimated hours )

2.2五折目標 The 50% target：所有模組以公允價值下限之 50% 為定價目標，並藉 §8 之公開協作程序持續校準。The 50% target: every module targets 50% of the fair-value floor, continuously calibrated through the open collaboration procedure in §8.

2.3最低收費 Minimum charge：每模組最低 HK$1,280。Minimum charge: each module carries a HK$1,280 minimum.

2.4工資下限 Wage floor：價格在任何情況下不低於法定最低工資乘以該模組之公開預估工時。現行法定最低工資為每小時 HK$43.10（2026 年 5 月 1 日起，勞工處）。各模組預估工時載於附錄 A，並隨每週基準一併公開。Wage floor: prices never fall below the statutory minimum wage (HK$43.10/hour from 1 May 2026, Labour Department) multiplied by the module's disclosed estimated hours (Appendix A), published with each weekly benchmark.

2.5品牌尾數調整（x,800／x,880／x80）只可向下或持平，且不得跌穿 §2.3 及 §2.4 之下限。Brand price-endings round at or below the formula output, never below the §2.3–2.4 floors.

2.6範圍與最低收費之一致性 Minimum-charge consistency：模組範圍須訂定至其同等範圍公允下限不低於 §2.3 之最低收費（例：修改請求定義為至 3 小時開發），以維持 §2.2 定價目標之一致性。Minimum-charge consistency: module scopes are defined so their equivalent-scope floor is never below the §2.3 minimum (e.g., a change request = up to 3 dev-hours), keeping the §2.2 pricing target consistent.

2.7不設月費 No recurring monthly fees：持續服務以定期預繳加購計劃（SiteCare+，24 個月）發售，按 §2.1 公式以多年期維護行情下限計價；只限項目上線時或其後 60 日內加購；期滿按當時基準價續期；可隨網站轉讓。No recurring monthly fees: ongoing service is sold as a fixed-term prepaid add-on (SiteCare+, 24 months) priced by §2.1 against multi-year maintenance floors; attachable only at launch or within 60 days; renewable at the then-current benchmark price; transferable with the site.

2.8計劃內承保服務費率 Covered-service rates under a plan：預繳計劃內之服務（如 SiteCare+ 會員修改請求，每小時 HK$140，不限次數，優先隊列）屬計劃承保範圍之服務費，並非獨立模組，故不適用 §2.3 每模組最低收費；惟仍須符合 §2.4 工資下限（$140/h ＞ $43.10/h ✓）。非計劃客戶之修改請求則為獨立模組，適用全部公式。Covered-service rates under a plan: services rendered under a prepaid plan (e.g., SiteCare+ member revisions at HK$140/hour, unlimited, priority queue) are covered-service fees, not standalone modules — the §2.3 module minimum does not apply, but the §2.4 wage floor still does ($140/h > $43.10/h ✓). Non-plan change requests are standalone modules subject to the full formula.

§3 定義§3 Definitions

3.1公允價值下限 Fair-value floor：就同等範圍服務，獨立公開香港報價區間之最低位。Fair-value floor: the lowest end of the independently published Hong Kong price range for equivalent scope.

3.2同等範圍 Equivalent scope（Q=1 訂製級）：同時符合以下全部條件之服務——(a) 全訂製程式碼，非模板或頁面產生器；(b) 原始碼完整交付；(c) AI-ready 技術文件；(d) 包含 CMS、基礎 SEO、SSL 及部署。Equivalent scope (Q=1, custom grade): a service meeting all of — (a) fully custom code, no templates/builders; (b) full source-code handover; (c) AI-ready documentation; (d) CMS, baseline SEO, SSL and deployment included.

3.3不符合 §3.2 之報價（模板方案、按用戶收費之 SaaS 授權）一律剔除，不作基準。Quotes failing §3.2 (template offers, per-user SaaS licences) are excluded from benchmarking.

§4 基準程序§4 Benchmark Procedure

4.1頻率 Cadence：逢星期一重新檢索及重算。價目表註明「基準更新」日期。Cadence: re-collected and recomputed every Monday; the rate card displays its refresh date.

4.2樣本 Sample：每一服務類別以不少於三個可信公開價格點建立區間。Sample: each service category requires ≥3 credible published price points.

4.3下限推導 Floor derivation：剔除不符 §3.2 之報價後，取符合同等範圍之最低公開報價為下限。Floor derivation: after §3.2 exclusions, the lowest remaining published quote becomes the floor.

4.4穩定帶 Stability band：下限變動少於 5% 不觸發調價，變動照錄於週報。Stability band: floor moves under 5% do not trigger repricing but are logged.

4.5報價鎖定 Quote lock：所有報價鎖定 14 日，以報價日與簽約日兩者中對客戶較優者為準。Quote lock: all quotes lock for 14 days at the client-favourable price.

§6 協作範圍及除外§6 Collaboration Scope & Exclusions

6.1§2 定價目標及 §8 定價協作邀請適用於全部服務模組，包括訂閱及專業審視服務；唯一除外見 §6.2。The §2 pricing target and the §8 collaboration invitation apply to all service modules, including subscriptions and professional-review services; the sole exclusion is §6.2.

6.2除外：AI 整合：採用專利申請中之專有技術（AI Logic Firewall），市場無同等服務可作比較，故不納入 §8 定價協作覆核；其價格仍按 §2 公式以參考基準計算並公開。Exclusion — AI integration: proprietary patent-pending IP with no market equivalent, hence outside the §8 collaborative review; its price still follows the §2 formula against a reference benchmark and is published.

6.3免費同行審視 Free peer review：本公司不提供收費合規服務。每位客戶獲一次免費商業政策同行審視，由 HKCGI 認證人員以同行經驗交流形式進行——並非顧問或法律意見；此項不收費，故不屬 §2 公式及本程序之範圍。Free peer review: we sell no paid compliance services. Every client receives one complimentary business-policy peer review by an HKCGI-credentialed professional, shared as peer experience — not consultancy or legal advice; being free, it sits outside the §2 formula and this procedure.

§7 紀錄及審計線索§7 Records & Audit Trail

7.1每週基準週報保留以下欄位：檢索日期、服務類別、來源名稱及連結、擷取價格、推導下限、對上週變動、是否觸發調價。Each weekly report retains: date, category, source name & link, captured price, derived floor, week-on-week change, reprice triggered (Y/N).

7.2價格變更紀錄格式：Change-log format:

日期Date 項目Item 舊下限Old floor 新下限New floor 變動Δ 新價New price
（每次調價一行）(one row per reprice)

7.3週報及變更紀錄保存不少於 24 個月，可應要求提供。Reports and change logs are retained ≥24 months and available on request.

§8 定價協作邀請§8 Pricing Collaboration Invitation

8.1定價唔係單方面聲明，而係一個開放程序：誠邀任何人（客戶、同行、公眾）提交同等範圍（§3.2）之香港市場價格資訊——不論高於或低於現行基準——連同來源出處。Pricing here is an open process, not a unilateral claim: anyone — clients, peers, the public — is invited to submit Hong Kong market pricing information for equivalent scope (§3.2), above or below the current benchmark, with its source.

8.2所有提交均於下一個每週更新內覆核；如成立，相應下限隨即調整，並記入變更紀錄及週報。Every submission is reviewed at the next weekly refresh; if valid, the floor adjusts and the change is logged in the change-log and weekly report.

8.3提交／聯絡：Submit / contact: vita@gentertainment.ltd

定價規則（方法學 v1.4）Pricing Rule (Methodology v1.4)

P = max( 0.50 × 公允下限, $1,280, $43.10 × 預估工時 )

  • 開放定價：0.5 × 下限為定價目標；誠邀任何人提交同等範圍行情資訊，一齊校準基準（方法學 §8）。Open pricing: 0.5 × the floor is the pricing target; anyone is invited to submit equivalent-scope market info to help calibrate it (Methodology §8).
  • SiteCare+ 加購計劃（24 個月預繳）：優先支援＋不限次數修改，會員價 $140/小時（承保服務費率，§2.8）。SiteCare+ add-on plan (24-month prepaid): priority support + unlimited revisions, $140/hour member rate (covered-service rate, §2.8).
  • 唯一協作覆核除外項：AI 整合（無可比對象）。不提供收費合規服務（免費同行審視除外）。The sole exclusion from collaborative review: AI integration (no market equivalent). No paid compliance services are offered (free peer review excepted).
  • 法定最低工資 $43.10/h（2026-05-01 起）。下限變動 <5% 不調價；報價鎖定 14 日。Statutory minimum wage $43.10/h (from 2026-05-01). Floor moves <5% don't reprice; quotes lock for 14 days.
  • 來源登記每週審視；每一擷取價格數字每週公開。將滑鼠移到「公允範圍」可見推導摘要。The source register is reviewed weekly; every captured price figure is published weekly. Hover the "Range" column to see the derivation summary.
